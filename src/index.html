She is a flower

A Poem by Shefali Dang

She is a flower

Holding herself together

By her roots

Using every drop

Of rain or tears

To make herself stronger

When she is ready

She will turn towards

The love shining on her

And open her petals

To reveal the Magnificent beauty

That she is
